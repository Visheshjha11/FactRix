About FactRix

Empowering truth through technology and community

Our Mission

FactRix was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to make fact-checking accessible to everyone. In today's digital age, misinformation spreads faster than ever, making it crucial to have reliable tools for verifying information.

Our AI-powered platform combines cutting-edge technology with community-driven verification to provide accurate, unbiased fact-checking services. We believe that access to truthful information is a fundamental right, and we're committed to making that right a reality.

By leveraging machine learning algorithms and human expertise, we create a robust system that helps users distinguish between fact and fiction, promoting a more informed and responsible digital community.
